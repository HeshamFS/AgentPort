# Code of Conduct

AgentPort contributors are expected to keep collaboration practical, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but ask for evidence when technical claims matter.
- Keep migration safety and user privacy central.
- Credit sources and prior work.
- Avoid marketing claims that the project does not yet support.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Posting private credentials, logs, or conversations.
- Misrepresenting compatibility or safety guarantees.
- Spamming maintainers or companies for attention.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that are unsafe or disruptive. Serious issues may result in temporary or permanent loss of contribution access.
